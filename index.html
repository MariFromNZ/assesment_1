about me

Hey there! Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

I pride myself on being a creative and optimistic individual with a friendly and engaging personality. Beyond work, I find solace in activities like hiking and immersing myself in nature. My strong communication skills, and diverse work background in research, education sales, and web application design, reflect my passion for continuous learning and aiming high.

After working as a scientist for 2 years, I realized that my true calling lies in a dynamic and innovative field where creativity plays a pivotal role—web design.

To prepare for this transition, I have undertaken various courses to enhance my skills. For more than a year I'm a member of The Interaction Design Foundation, where I've completed Masterclasses and some UX courses.

With hands-on experience gained through real projects for New Zealand companies (totaling 1.5 years), I've honed my skills in wireframing, prototyping, information architecture, usability testing, UX research, and graphic design. I utilize various tools such as Visual Studio Code, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, and Figma.

On a broader scale, I embody strong communication skills and a diverse work background encompassing research, education, sales, and web application design. My passion for continuous learning and setting ambitious goals is the driving force behind my journey.

Thank you for joining me on this exciting venture, and I look forward to the opportunities that lie ahead.
